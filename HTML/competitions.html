Past Events & Awards

2x Connect Award

In the Into the Deep season of FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC), the Connect Award is given to a team that connects with their local STEM community and helps others learn about FIRST. Our team has won this award at the FiT-Central HSATigers and the FiT-Central Semi-Area Championship.

Finalist Alliance

The Finalist Award recognizes strong strategy, teamwork, and adaptability. At the FiT-Central Semi-Area Championship, our team, ICBC ITKAN Ravenbots (#27859), earned this award, proving we could compete with the best.

2nd Place Alliance

At the LightSaders Rookie Invitational, our team was part of the 2nd Place Alliance, finishing as runners-up in the elimination rounds. Securing this spot proved our ability to compete at a high level, even as a rookie team.
