Welcome to our robotics journey

Our Mission

Based in Cedar Park, Texas, Iron Ummah is rooted in the Islamic Center of Brushy Creek community. Our mission is to bring people together through competition while inspiring the next generation in STEM.

We are a team of passionate and dedicated students committed to excellence in FIRST Tech Challenge and to give back to our community.

Team Highlights

2x Connect Award Winner

Recognized for connecting with our local STEM community and helping others learn about FIRST.

Finalist Alliance

Achieved Finalist status at the FiT-Central Semi-Area Championship, competing with the best teams.

2nd Place Alliance

Secured 2nd place at the LightSaders Rookie Invitational, proving our competitive capabilities.

OPR Over 90

Our robot has achieved an Offensive Power Rating of over 90, demonstrating strong performance.
