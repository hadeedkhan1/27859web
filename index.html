Welcome to Iron Ummah #27859

Competing with Robots

Our Community

Based in Cedar Park, Texas, Iron Ummah is rooted in the Islamic Center of Brushy Creek community. Our aim is to bring people together with robotics.

We are a team of extremely passionate and dedicated students who are committed to becoming the best in robotics.

Team Highlights

A few of the milestones we’re proud of.

01

2x Connect Award Winner

Recognized for connecting with our local STEM community and helping others learn about FIRST.

02

Finalist Alliance

Achieved Finalist status at the FiT-Central Semi-Area Championship, competing with the best teams.

03

2nd Place Alliance

Secured 2nd place at the LightSaders Rookie Invitational, proving our competitive capabilities.

04

OPR Over 90

Our robot has achieved an Offensive Power Rating of over 90, demonstrating strong performance.
