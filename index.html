About ICBC Ravenbots

Robotics Excellence

We design, build, and program competitive robots for FIRST Tech Challenge competitions, pushing the boundaries of innovation and engineering.

Team Collaboration

Our diverse team of students works together to solve complex challenges, developing skills in engineering, programming, and leadership.

Competitive Success

With multiple awards including Connect Awards and Finalist Alliance recognition, we're proud of our achievements in FTC competitions.

Team Highlights

2x Connect Award Winner

Recognized for connecting with our local STEM community and helping others learn about FIRST.

Finalist Alliance

Achieved Finalist status at the FiT-Central Semi-Area Championship, competing with the best teams.

2nd Place Alliance

Secured 2nd place at the LightSaders Rookie Invitational, proving our competitive capabilities.

OPR Over 90

Our robot has achieved an Offensive Power Rating of over 90, demonstrating strong performance.
